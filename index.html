It is no secret that movies are among the most common forms of entertainment and more so they cut across all age groups. Naturally going to the movies is a hobby shared by many, our beloved Kampala being no exception. This however becomes tricky in the case that one wants to view the catalogue and showtimes of their favourite new films because there is no formally established manner of browsing nearby cinema content be it coming soon or already showing and their ticket prices. One might argue that that isnt a big deal, however things only get worse when movie-goers try to book a ticket without actually going through the inconvenience of travelling to the cinema. This limits one to either watch on-the-spot or go back to running errands and risk missing the opening scenes of the movie. Owing to the above stated problem, there is a need for a more reliable alternative to booking tickets on site.

Entertainment for everyone!

The whole aim of our app is to allow people to view the movies showing at a given cinema on a given day of the week and the show times. The app will enable a user to know the seat number upon arrival at the cinema. The app will also incorporate a ticket reservation and payment method to go along with it. The app will also simplify the whole process of planning a movie date. In addition to the fun, users will be able to buy movies on demand.

CinemApp is the new movie and theatre app where everyone can catch the movies showing in theatres of their choice, purchase movies on demand and have much more fun at their own convinience.
