Entertainment for everyone!

Install the app onto your mobile devices from either app store or google play store. At first launch, the app will take you to the landing screen where it will require you to signin using your email and other details, or using google authentication then there will be another option of logging in as a guest which will not require to capture any of your personal details. After you signin, you can now login to your centre of entertainment. Here, you will be able to view the "Now Showing" shows from which you can choose your favorite to catch at the cinema. You can then go ahead to see more details about the specific show you have chosen and also go ahead to book a specific seat(s) to watch from while in the cinema hall or theatre where you can select seat numbers according to physical location and also chhose your convinient day of the week and time of the day according to the available ones. One can then go ahead to view the details of their ticket icluding the show, time, date and number of seats not forgetting the affordable amount to pay for the cinema which comes with a qrcode for verification of the ticket while at the cinema. You can there after go ahead to make the pending payments for the cinema.

Movies Shop

For the other section of entertainment, our app has an online movies shop with the latest movies on demand by movie lovers. Here, movies that are uploaded by users and cinemas can be accessed by other users in the shop screen and their details can be farther viewed by the users and also be addeed to the user's cart for shopping, in the cart screen, a user can then purchase the movies they want to have by making the online payment and wait to have fun at their own convinience. A user also has a personal home page in the app which is their profile page where their details are seen and can easily access their cart and wishlist directly from there and also can easily opt out of the app by logging out. This is all like this to ensure that our app is easy to access and to use by anyone who has an access to a mobile device and the internet. Making us the best home of entertainment.
